LatticeRT

LatticeRT is a browser-based research tool to load CT + RTSTRUCT DICOMs locally, generate sphere lattices, and export a derived RTSTRUCT—no data leaves your machine.

⚠️ Important Notice

Research only. Not validated for clinical use.
